Who is MATOMELA GROUP NO LIMIT

Matomela Group No Limit is a 100% Black-owned company established and registered in 2024. We are driven by experienced directors and partners in the security industry, with a mission to bridge gaps in private security and help create safer communities.

Professional. Disciplined. Community-Focused.
